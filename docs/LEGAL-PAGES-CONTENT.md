## Refund & Cancellation Policy (summary)
- Free tier: no charges.
- Paid tiers: billed monthly, 6-month, or annual per user choice.
- Cancellation: you can cancel any time; access continues until period end; no automatic refunds for unused time unless required by law.
- Cooling-off: provide best-effort goodwill within 7 days for annual/6-month plans if no meaningful usage (admin review).

- Disputes: contact billing@thebayislands.au before initiating chargebacks.
