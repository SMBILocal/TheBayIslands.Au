### Every Article Should Include:
1. **Meta Tags**:
   - `<title>` (50-60 chars) â€” Keyword-focused
   - `<meta description>` (150-160 chars) â€” Compelling call-to-action
   - `<meta keywords>` â€” 5-7 primary keywords

2. **Headings Structure** (H1â€“H3):
   - One H1 per page (article title)
   - H2 for main sections
   - H3 for subsections
   - Keyword-rich headings

3. **Internal Linking**:
   - Link to related articles
   - Link to island guides
   - Link to relevant service pages (jobs, directory, events, classifieds)
   - 3-5 internal links per 1000 words minimum

4. **JSON-LD Schema**:
   - Article schema (author, datePublished, dateModified)
   - BreadcrumbList for navigation
   - LocalBusiness schema for locations
   - FAQPage schema for Q&A content

5. **Content Length**:
   - 1500â€“2500 words per article
