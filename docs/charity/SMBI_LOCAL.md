# SMBI Local / SMBI Local Ltd (Charity)

## Who we are
- SMBI Local (smbilocal.au) is the community-led group powering thebayislands.au.
- SMBI Local Ltd (charitable vehicle) manages commercial transactions, fundraising, and donation intake to benefit the Southern Moreton Bay Islands community.

## Mission
- Serve local residents and businesses with trustworthy information, listings, and opportunities.
- Reinforce community resilience by "serving each other in the SMBI to help each other" and building a stronger Bay Islands network.

## Ways to support
- **Donate funds:** contribute to operating costs and community initiatives.
- **Donate prizes/gift cards:** sponsor monthly raffles; prizes may include store vouchers, hampers, or donated items.
- **Volunteer time/skills:** content curation, moderation, outreach, events.
- **Offer services:** discounts or pro bono work for local causes.

## Raffles & fundraising
- Monthly raffles with tickets available via the Donate page.
- Prizes: local store gift cards, hampers, and donated items from community partners.
- Businesses can pledge prizes or vouchers via the donation form.

## Governance & accountability
- Transparent allocation of funds toward community outcomes and platform upkeep.
- Separation of concerns: SMBI Local operates the product; SMBI Local Ltd handles financial transactions and donations.
- Contact: donations@smbilocal.au

## How to get involved
- Visit the Donate page to contribute funds or pledge prizes.
- Email donations@smbilocal.au to volunteer or discuss partnerships.
- Share the platform and help onboard local businesses.
