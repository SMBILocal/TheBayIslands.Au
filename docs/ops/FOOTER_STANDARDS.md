# Footer Standards (2026-ready)

Purpose: ensure a modern, compliant, SEO-friendly footer for thebayislands.au with clear navigation, trust signals, and acknowledgements.

## Required Columns
1) Explore: Directory, Articles, Events, Classifieds, Jobs.
2) Account: Login, Sign up, Upgrade & Pricing, Sitemap.
3) Policies: Terms, Privacy, Refund & Cancellation, Cookies, Environmental.
4) Community & Support: Donate, About SMBI Local, Contact/Support.

## Acknowledgement of Country
- Text: acknowledge the Traditional Custodians of the lands and waters of Southern Moreton Bay Islands, often known as Canaipa/Kanopa (Russell Island), and pay respect to Elders past and present.
- Placement: below columns, above copyright line.

## Ownership & Attribution
- "thebayislands.au is created and operated by SMBI Local (smbilocal.au)."
- Note the charity vehicle: "Commercial transactions and fundraising are managed by SMBI Local Ltd (charitable)."
- Include a Donate link to the dedicated page.

## SEO & Compliance
- Include Sitemap link.
- Ensure all policy links are crawlable.
- Use accessible contrast and semantic `<footer>`.
- Keep link text descriptive (no "click here").

## Optional Enhancements
- Social links (if available) with accessible labels.
- Newsletter signup (when privacy and consent flows are ready).
- Microcopy for support: support@thebayislands.au.
